How To Install

  1. Click below to download the zip archive with the captions inside
  2. Extract the closecaption_english.dat and closecaption_english.txt files from the ZIP archive
  3. Place them in the following folder: C:\Program Files\Steam\steamapps\<username>\team fortress 2\tf\resource\ Replace <username> with your steam log-in user name
  4. Open Team Fortress 2
  5. Go to the Options menu
  6. Go to the Audio menu
  7. Click the drop-down for Captions and select an option as follows:
    1. If you want to have all possible sounds, including grunts of pain and laughter, choose "Closed Captions"
    2. If you want to have only actual spoken lines, choose "Subtitles"
  8. Click the "OK" button to confirm the change
  9. Play the game with your new captions!
