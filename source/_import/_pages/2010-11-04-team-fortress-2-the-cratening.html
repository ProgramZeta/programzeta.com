Goals

  • Create an entertaining event
  • Get a few bits of rare data assigned to my digital avatar in some game
  • Become familiar with how YouTube works from the publishing side
  • Become familiar with movie editing and production
  • Become familiar with LiveStream and how it works

Summary

On October 30, 2010 I ran an event wherein I opened fifty Mann Co. Supply Crates in Valve's Team Fortress 2 at one time. With a full 32-player server as well as over 20 people viewing a live video feed, I proceeded to open the crates only to find no unusual hats. It was a great learning experience, despite the technical problems and heartbreak. I plan to run a "sequel" event with a more streamlined experience.
